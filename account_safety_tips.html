🔒 Account Security Tips

How to Create a Strong Password?

  • ✅ Use at least 12 characters, including letters, numbers, and symbols.
  • 🚫 Avoid using common words or personal information.
  • 🔄 Change your password regularly.
  • 🛡 Use a password manager to store your passwords securely.

How to Enable Two-Factor Authentication?

  • 🔹 Go to your account security settings.
  • 🔹 Select "Two-Factor Authentication".
  • 🔹 Choose a verification method like SMS or an authentication app.
  • 🔹 Confirm activation and save backup codes.

How to Avoid Phishing Attacks?

  • 🚫 Do not click on suspicious links in emails or messages.
  • 🔍 Always check the URL before entering your credentials.
  • 🛡 Use security software and antivirus protection.
  • 🔄 Never share your login details with anyone.
