📢 Cyber Awareness Section

Common Cyber Threats:

  • 🛑 *Phishing* – Fake emails or messages tricking users into giving personal data.
  • 💀 *Malware* – Viruses, ransomware, and spyware that can harm your device.
  • 🔗 *Social Engineering* – Hackers pretending to be trusted sources to steal information.
  • 📡 *Wi-Fi Attacks* – Using fake Wi-Fi networks to steal login credentials.

How to Avoid Online Scams?

  • 🔍 *Verify Websites* – Always check the URL before entering your details.
  • 🚫 *Avoid Suspicious Links* – Don't click on unknown links in emails or social media.
  • 🔒 *Use Secure Payment Methods* – Prefer credit cards over direct bank transfers.
  • 🛡 *Enable Alerts* – Set up fraud alerts on your banking accounts.

How to Stay Safe Online?

  • 🔹 *Use Strong Passwords* – Combine letters, numbers, and symbols.
  • 🔹 *Enable Two-Factor Authentication* – Adds an extra security layer.
  • 🔹 *Keep Software Updated* – Security patches fix vulnerabilities.
  • 🔹 *Be Cautious with Public Wi-Fi* – Avoid logging into accounts on public networks.
